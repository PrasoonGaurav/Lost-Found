Hello Visitors, we the Google Student Community of DIT University, aim high at being focused on building relationships with our clients and community. Our vision is to make an impact on the society by giving the best out of what we have, as a team. We vow to unite together and join our hands to stop the immense wastage of food. With our recently launched Web-Project, "Better Pledge" we solemnly undertake the resolution to pool the left over(the waste food) and the left out (the needy people), hence establishing an amicable solution to address food waste. Let's not be the worst food waste culprit in the world. A lot of that veritable waste can be a blessing to the living lot. Here's what we can do about it. Earnestly urging all NGOs, hoteliers, food geeks, families, children and individuals to embark on this momentous journey of the "Better Pledge" so that we waste not what we want not.
